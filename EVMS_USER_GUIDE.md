# EVMS Quick User Guide - Professional Executive Visit Management

## 🎯 Quick Start: Creating an Executive Visit

### Step 1: Create the Visit
1. Go to **EVMS Dashboard** or **Visits**
2. Click **"Add Visitor"** or **"Create Visit"**
3. Fill in:
   - Visit Name (e.g., "CEO Site Inspection Q3 2026")
   - Start Date & End Date
   - Purpose & Host Company
4. Click **Save**

### Step 2: Add Visitors (The VIPs)
1. Open your visit
2. Go to **Visitors** tab
3. Click **"Add Visitor"**
4. Enter:
   - Name, Designation, Department
   - Country, Email, Phone
   - Arrival & Departure dates (optional)
   - Travel details (flight numbers, airports, times)
5. Add all visitors
6. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Add Hosts (Your Team)
1. Go to **Hosts** tab
2. Click **"Add Host"**
3. Enter:
   - Name, Designation, Department
   - Email, Phone
   - Company Name
   - Mark as "Company Head" if applicable
4. Add all hosts
5. Click **Save**

### Step 4: Build Visit Timeline ⭐ (The Main Event)
1. Click **"Visit Timeline"** in the menu OR
2. Click the Timeline tab in your visit
3. Click **"Create Timeline"**

**For Each Meeting:**
1. Select **"Meeting"** type
2. Choose:
   - **Date** (required)
   - **Start Time** (required, 12-hour format)
   - **End Time** (required)
   - **Visitors** attending (required - click names)
   - **Hosts** attending (required - click names)
   - **Description** (required) - This is your meeting name!
     - Examples: "Civil Discussion", "OHL Progress Review", "Safety Walkthrough", "Project Kickoff"

**For Each Activity:**
1. Select **"Activity"** type
2. Choose:
   - **Activity Type** (required) - Select from dropdown or type custom
     - Examples: "Airport Pickup", "Lunch", "Dinner", "Tea Break", "Site Visit"
   - **Date** (required)
   - **Start Time** (required)
   - **End Time** (required)
   - **Visitors** (required)
   - **Hosts** (optional for activities)
   - **Description** (optional notes)

3. Click **"Add Timeline Item"** to add more items
4. Click **"Save Complete Timeline"**

## ✏️ Edit & Reorder Timeline

### Edit Any Item
1. Click the **✏️ Edit** button on any timeline item
2. Modify times, dates, or description
3. Click **Save** (or Cancel)

### Delete Any Item
1. Click the **🗑️ Delete** button
2. Confirm deletion

### Drag & Drop to Reorder 🔥
1. **Hover** over any timeline item
2. Look for the **⋮⋮** grip handle on the left
3. **Click and drag** the item:
   - Drag **up/down** to reorder within the same day
   - Drag to **different day section** to move between days
4. Release - it auto-saves!

**Perfect for last-minute schedule changes!**

## 📅 Calendar View

1. Go to **EVMS → Calendar**
2. The calendar automatically opens on your visit's month (no scrolling!)
3. Color-coded items:
   - 🟦 Blue = Visits
   - 🟪 Purple = Meetings
   - 🟣 Violet = Activities
4. Click any item to view details

## 📄 Export Professional PDF/PNG

### From Dashboard or Visits Page:
1. Find your visit in the list
2. Click the **"Export"** dropdown button
3. Choose:
   - **Export PDF** - Multi-page PDF for long visits
   - **Export PNG** - High-quality image
   - **Print** - Direct browser print

### From Visit Detail Page:
1. Open your visit
2. Click **"Export"** at the top
3. Select format
4. Also available: **"Download Template"** - Clean executive format

**The exported document shows:**
- Professional header with visit details
- Visitors list with photos/initials
- Travel details (if common for all)
- Host company details
- **Day-wise timeline** (Day 1, Day 2, Day 3...)
- Footer with generation date/time

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Meeting Descriptions
Use clear, concise descriptions since there's no separate title:
- ✅ Good: "Civil Discussion", "OHL Progress Review", "Safety Walkthrough"
- ❌ Avoid: "Meeting about the civil aspects and other things"

### Activity Types
Choose from predefined types or create custom:
- **Predefined:** Airport Pickup, Lunch, Dinner, Tea Break, Hotel Check-in, Site Visit, etc.
- **Custom:** Type anything you need

### Time Format
- All times use **12-hour format** (AM/PM)
- Dropdowns for easy selection:
  - Hour: 01-12
  - Minute: 00, 15, 30, 45
  - Period: AM/PM

### Multi-Day Visits
- For 3-day visit: Timeline shows Day 1, Day 2, Day 3
- For 5-day visit: Timeline shows Day 1 through Day 5
- Export automatically splits by day

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Where did the Meeting Schedule page go?**
A: It's been replaced by the unified **Visit Timeline**. All meeting creation happens there now, alongside activities.

**Q: Why don't I see Meeting Title or Location fields?**
A: We simplified! The **Description** field is your meeting title (e.g., "Civil Discussion"). Location isn't needed for the timeline view.

**Q: Can I create standalone meetings without a visit?**
A: No - all meetings must be part of a visit. This keeps everything organized.

**Q: What if I need to change the schedule last minute?**
A: **Drag and drop!** Just grab the grip handle and move items around. It saves automatically.

**Q: Can I have different activity types?**
A: Yes! Use the dropdown for common ones, or click "type custom activity type" to create your own.

**Q: How do I know what day each item is on?**
A: The timeline automatically groups items with day headers: "DAY 1", "DAY 2", etc.

**Q: Why are Hosts optional for activities but required for meetings?**
A: Activities like "Airport Pickup" or "Lunch" might not need hosts. Meetings always need at least one host from your team.

**Q: Can I export a specific day only?**
A: Not yet - export generates the full visit timeline. You can use the Calendar view to see specific days.

**Q: What if my export dropdown is cut off?**
A: It's been fixed! The dropdown now has proper z-index and appears above all content.

## 🎓 Best Practices

1. **Create visitors and hosts BEFORE building timeline** - Makes selection easier
2. **Use consistent naming** - "Civil Discussion" not "Civil disc." or "Meeting about civil"
3. **Add travel details to visitors** - Automatically shows in template
4. **Mark company heads** - Highlights them in exports
5. **Use short names for visitors** - Displays better in timeline (e.g., "John S." instead of "John Smith")
6. **Build timeline chronologically** - Easier to review
7. **Export after finalizing** - Share PDF with stakeholders
8. **Use drag-drop for changes** - Faster than editing times manually
